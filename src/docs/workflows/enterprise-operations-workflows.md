# Enterprise Operations Workflows

## Procurement

Purchase requests are created in a pending approval state. They can be approved, rejected, or cancelled. Purchase orders can be created directly or linked to a purchase request, then approved, rejected, or cancelled.

## Inventory And Stock

Inventory items store current, reserved, available, and reorder-level quantities. Stock movements record stock in, stock out, adjustment, reservation, and release actions.

## Maintenance

Maintenance records support preventive and corrective work. Records can be requested, scheduled, moved in progress, completed, or cancelled.

## Service Tickets

Service tickets capture customer issues, assignment, priority, status, resolution, comments, and history through persisted ticket updates.

## Notifications And Audit

Notifications store user-facing events such as approvals, low stock, maintenance due, and ticket assignment. Audit logs store entity, action, old values, new values, user, and timestamp metadata.
